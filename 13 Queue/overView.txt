Adding and removing of elements in queue should be from opposite end
element added first will remove first


FIFO -> First In First Out

  dequeue() <--  [11| 22| 33| 44| 55]  <-- enqueue()
                front             end


operations: 
1. enqueue(data)
2. dequeue()
3. getFront()
4. getRear()
5. size()
6. isEmpty()
